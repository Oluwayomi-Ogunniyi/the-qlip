Cookie Policy

Transparency in every byte: How we use cookies to enhance your experience.

Last Updated: November 08, 2025

1. Introduction

At The Qlip ("we," "us," or "our"), a technology company based in Lagos, Nigeria, we respect your privacy and are committed to transparent data practices. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website (theqclip.com) and in our services, in compliance with the EU ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, Nigeria's Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, and other applicable laws.

Cookies help us improve functionality, analyze usage, and personalize content. This policy is part of our Privacy Policy. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described, subject to your preferences via our cookie banner.

For questions, contact privacy@theqclip.com.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They enable sites to remember your actions and preferences over time, improving your experience. We also use similar technologies like web beacons, pixels, and local storage.

Session Cookies: Temporary, deleted when you close your browser. Persistent Cookies: Remain until expiry or deletion. We do not use cookies for tracking across unrelated sites without consent.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We classify cookies by purpose and classify them as follows, in line with GDPR/NDPA guidelines. You can manage non-essential cookies via our consent banner.

We do not use cookies for AI training or sharing with unauthorized third parties. See our Privacy Policy for data processing details.

4. How We Use Third-Party Cookies

We partner with trusted providers:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymized traffic insights (IP masked per GDPR).
  • Mailchimp: For newsletter tracking (with opt-out).
  • Font Awesome/CDNJS: For icons and libraries (essential, no tracking).

Third parties must comply with our contracts and applicable laws. We do not allow cross-site tracking without consent.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You control your cookies:

  • Our Banner: On first visit, select preferences (e.g., accept analytics only). Update anytime via the footer "Cookie Settings" link.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers let you block, delete, or notify for cookies. Note: Disabling may impair site functionality.
  • Do Not Track (DNT): We honor DNT signals where possible.
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use services like YourAdChoices or NDPC resources for broader controls.

Changes apply to future visits; clear cache for immediate effect.

6. Cookies in Our Services

For free tools (e.g., invoice generator) and project modals, we use session cookies for temporary data. No persistent tracking without consent. Mobile apps may use similar device identifiers, disclosed in app privacy notices.

7. International Transfers and Compliance

Cookies from global providers (e.g., Google in the US) involve transfers safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) under NDPA Section 42 and GDPR. We conduct transfer impact assessments for high-risk cases.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for legal or technical reasons. Changes will be posted here with the new "Last Updated" date. For material updates, we'll notify via banner or email. Continued use means acceptance.
