Our Story

Founded in 2025, The Qlip emerged from a shared passion for bridging the gap between groundbreaking ideas, seamless digital execution and tech literacy. What started as a small team of visionary developers in Lagos has grown into a global force in software engineering, AI innovation, and multimedia mastery.

We've partnered with startups, enterprises, and creatives to deliver multiple projects, from AI-driven automations to immersive branding experiences. At our core, we're not just builders... we're enablers, turning complex challenges into elegant solutions that scale with ambition.
